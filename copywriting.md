# Maison B-Sun - COPYWRITING.md

This file turns the brochure content into web-ready copy for the Maison B-Sun website.

It is based on the brochure text in `design.pdf`, adapted to the current one-page site structure, and aligned with the brochure-led visual direction described in `DESIGN.md`.

## 1. Copy goals

- Sound warm, sunny, calm, and clear.
- Feel like a refined holiday brochure, not a booking platform.
- Stay concise and easy to scan on the web.
- Let the photography do the emotional work.
- Keep the writing factual, elegant, and reassuring.

## 2. Brand voice

- **Tone:** chaleureux, lumineux, simple, accueillant.
- **Style:** phrases courtes à moyennes, fluides, jamais trop commerciales.
- **Promise:** une maison rénovée, confortable, facile à vivre, entre village, collines et mer.
- **Avoid:** superlatifs creux, ton trop luxueux, formulations trop techniques, répétitions inutiles.

## 12. Notes for implementation

- Prefer short paragraphs and generous spacing.
- Use factual highlights instead of long descriptive blocks where possible.
- Let room specifications stay precise and compact.
- Keep the contact section highly visible and easy to scan.
- Preserve the warm brochure tone, but simplify for web reading.
